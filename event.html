📝 Travel program

🗓 FRIDAY, MAY 31, 2024

Departure from Bari and surrounding areas at 11:00 PM.Overnight journey with various technical stops

🗓 SATURDAY, JUNE 1, 2024

Arrival at a 4-star hotel and luggage accommodation in a room reserved for us, then heading to Mugello to attend the practice sessions.

Mugello is a magical place for motorbikes, nestled in a location of rare beauty.The Tuscan hills, the winding curves, and the perpetual ups and downs in the greenery make it a true temple for fans.

For our colors, it is the home Grand Prix, and the spectacle on the meadows is incredible. Return to the hotel after the race.

Breakfast from 7:00 AM to 8:00 AM.Departure for Mugello for the MotoGP Grand Prix, the most awaited and loved event by the fans.

Return at the end of the race with arrival in the evening.
